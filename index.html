The Canadian Caregiver Assessment and Resources Tool

Caregiving is a significant and challenging endeavour, and we understand that it can sometimes feel overwhelming. However, you are not navigating this path alone. A wealth of resources is at your fingertips to support and guide you through your caregiving journey. 

The Canadian Caregiver Assessment and Resources Tool (C-CART) is designed to be both thorough and time-efficient, typically requiring just 10 to 15 minutes to complete, depending on your unique responses. Upon completion, you'll receive a personalized list of resources, specifically curated to meet the needs of caregivers in your local area.

To ensure you receive the most accurate and tailored resource recommendations, we encourage you to answer all the questions in the assessment fully. Please keep in mind that incomplete responses could lead to less specific suggestions, which might not completely align with your unique caregiving needs. Your detailed input is important in helping us provide you with the most effective support and resources.
