The Canadian Caregiver Assessment and Resources Tool
Caregiving is stressful. Fortunately, there are resources available across Canada to help. You are about to complete the Canadian Caregiver Assessment and Resources Tool (C-CART). This assessment will take approximately 10 minutes to complete, and then you will receive a list of resources that are available in your community.

Note: None of your personal information (i.e. name, birthday etc.) is collected through this self-assessment. The responses on this self-assessment are stored in a secure server for quality improvement.
